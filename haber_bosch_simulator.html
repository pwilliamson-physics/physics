Equilibrium Conversion vs Temperature — find the optimum!
🔥
Temperature effect
Reaction is exothermic (−92 kJ/mol). Lower T shifts equilibrium right → more NH₃. But rate becomes very slow — a direct trade-off.
💨
Pressure effect
4 mol gas → 2 mol gas. Higher P shifts equilibrium right → more NH₃ and also increases rate. Both effects favour high P — but plant costs rise steeply.
♻️
Why recycle?
Single-pass conversion is only 15–65% depending on conditions. Unreacted H₂ and N₂ are recycled back to the Converter, achieving near-total conversion of the fresh feed.
🎯
Industrial optimum
Typically 400–500 °C and 150–300 atm over iron catalyst (+ K₂O + Al₂O₃ promoters). Use the sliders to discover this optimum yourself!
