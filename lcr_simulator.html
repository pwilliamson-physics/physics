🔄 Phasor Diagram (I = 1 A reference)

I — reference current (horizontal)
VR — across resistor, in phase with I
VL — across inductor, +90° ahead of I
VC — across capacitor, −90° behind I
Vtotal — total supply voltage (vector sum)
At resonance, VL and VC are equal and opposite — they cancel. Vtotal = VR and φ = 0°. The circuit acts like a pure resistor.
